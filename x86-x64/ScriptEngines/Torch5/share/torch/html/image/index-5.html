image.crop(src,dst,x1,y1,x2,y2)

Torch Manual  >   Image Package Reference Manual  >   image.crop(src,dst,x1,y1,x2,y2)
image.crop(src,dst,x1,y1,x2,y2) crops the Tensor src by taking only the part of the image with top left-hand corner (x1,y1) and bottom right corner (x2,y2) to fit the destination Tensor dst.

If dst is not of size (x2-x1+1,y2-y1+1) then the cropped part of the image is also scaled to fit the dimensions of dst.

src and dst cannot be the same.
