painter.depth

Torch Manual  >   QtWidget Package Reference Manual  >   QtLua Classes  >   qt.QtLuaPainter  >   QtLuaPainter Properties  >   painter.depth

Expression painter.depth returns the color depth of the target surface for drawings. A depth of 1 indicates a bitonal surface. A depth of 24 or 32 indicates a true color surface.
